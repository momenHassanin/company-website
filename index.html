Advanced Marketing and Sales Skills

  • Training Course
  • Course Objective
  • Topics
This course is designed for professionals working in the different fields of marketing and sales, public relations, and operations, in addition to those wishing to acquire more advanced marketing and sales skills and competencies, as the course aims to provide participants with the skills and knowledge that enable them to determine and set the activities and practices of the sales and marketing processes, execute effective marketing audit and control processes, set successful marketing plans, Enabling participants to achieve their marketing objectives through digital technologies and enabling the marketing activities of the participants to achieve profitable acquisitions and retentions of their target audience within a multichannel buying process and customer lifecycle, and master the selling process and develop sales opportunity plans to maximize sales revenues and profitability.
  • Provide participants with the advanced marketing and sales skills and competencies.
  • Provide participants with the skills and knowledge that enable them to determine and set the activities and practices of the sales and marketing processes.
  • Enable participants to execute effective marketing audit and control processes and set successful marketing plans.
  • Enable participants to achieve their marketing objectives through digital technologies and enabling the marketing activities of the participants to achieve profitable acquisitions and retentions of their target audience within a multichannel buying process and customer lifecycle.
  • Enable participants to master the selling process and develop sales opportunity plans to maximize sales revenues and profitability.
  1. Introduction to Sales and Marketing
  2. Sales and Marketing Practices
  3. Marketing Environment and Marketing Analysis Techniques
  4. Strategic Marketing Plan and Marketing Audit
  5. Traditional and Digital Marketing
  6. The Buying & Selling Processes and Sales Opportunity Planning
  7. Building Strong Business Relationships
  8. Marketing and Sales Effectiveness and Balanced Scorecard
